Protocol Zero: The Digital Disappearance Guide

Version 1.0 | FreeState Gear

Phase 1: The Phone (The Primary Tracker)

Your smartphone is the biggest liability. Here is how to lock it down.

  1. Disable Advertising ID: Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn Off.
  2. Location Services: Turn OFF for everything except Maps. set Maps to "While Using".
  3. Microphone Access: Revoke access for Facebook, Instagram, and unauthorized apps.

Phase 2: The Browser (The Window)

Stop using Chrome. It is a data vacuum.

  • Brave Browser: Built-in ad blocking and tracker blocking.
  • DuckDuckGo: Search engine that doesn't log your queries.
  • VPN: Use ProtonVPN (Swiss-based) for all traffic.

Phase 3: The Money (The Paper Trail)

Digital payments are tracked forever.

  • Cash is King: Use cash for all local transactions (groceries, gas).
  • Privacy.com: Use virtual cards for online purchases to mask your real bank details.

Conclusion

Privacy is a habit, not a product. Stay vigilant.
